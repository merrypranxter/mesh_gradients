# Visual Targets

## 1. Stripe Mesh
6 pastel control points, slow drift, minimal warp. Soft, friendly, commercial. The kind of gradient you'd see on a Stripe landing page. Colors flow seamlessly without ever going grey.

## 2. Liquid Blob
4 points, heavy IDW, strong domain warp. Lava-lamp aesthetic. The colors blob and merge organically. You can almost feel the surface tension.

## 3. Aurora
Vertical bands of color, OKLab interpolation, slow vertical drift. `['#00ff9d','#00d4ff','#9d00ff','#ff00d4']`. Ethereal, sky-like, hypnotic.

## 4. Holo Foil
Hue-cycling metallic sheen with iridescence hook from `thin_film_iridescence`. The colors shift like oil on water, like a holographic sticker.

## 5. Acid Mesh
8+ points, maximalist neon, fast warp. The chaos-goblin special. Every color at once, moving fast, unapologetic.
